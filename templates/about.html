The Challenge

Nutrition gaps in Karnataka schools

Many school children face nutritional challenges such as undernutrition, poor dietary awareness, and imbalanced meal consumption. Schools often lack simple tools to address these issues.

  • ⚠️ No easy way to assess BMI and track nutritional risk across students
  • ⚠️ Teachers struggle to understand nutrition gaps without clinical expertise
  • ⚠️ No structured way to recommend balanced meals using local, affordable foods
  • ⚠️ Parents receive little nutrition education in a language they understand

Why it matters

Early nutritional intervention improves cognitive development, school attendance, and long-term health outcomes. Karnataka's diversity in diet and region makes a one-size-fits-all approach ineffective — NutriPrint is built to be regional and personal.

Our Solution

Everything a school needs in one platform

Eight integrated features that take schools from data to action.

📐
BMI Assessment
Accurate BMI calculation with age-gender-specific percentile classification and personalised advice.
🤖
AI Nutrition Advisor
Groq-powered conversational advisor that answers nutrition questions in English and Kannada.
🗓️
Weekly Meal Planning
AI-generated 7-day meal plans tailored to age group, diet type, region, and nutrition strategy.
📊
Nutrition Gap Analysis
Identifies shortfalls in calories, protein, calcium, and iron against age-appropriate targets.
⬜
QR Recipe System
Scan QR codes on meal plan items to open detailed recipe pages with ingredients and steps.
🏅
Nutrition Score
Automated weekly nutrition scoring system that grades meal plan quality from A to F.
🖨️
Printable Posters
Exportable infographic health posters and PDF reports ready for classroom display and parent sharing.
🗣️
Bilingual Support
Full English and ಕನ್ನಡ interface so teachers and parents engage in their preferred language.

Technology Stack

Built on a modern production stack

Each layer is chosen for reliability, speed, and developer experience.

🌐
Frontend
HTML · CSS · JavaScript
Tailwind CSS utility classes, Jinja2 templating, vanilla JS with no heavy framework dependency.
⚡
Backend
Python · FastAPI
Async REST API with Pydantic validation, structured routers, and Supabase integration.
🗄️
Database
Supabase · PostgreSQL
Managed Postgres with row-level security, real-time capabilities, and built-in auth.
🤖
AI Engine
Groq API
Ultra-fast LLM inference for meal plan generation, nutrition advice, and bilingual responses.
🚀
Deployment
Render
Continuous deployment from Git with automatic HTTPS, environment variables, and zero-config scaling.
