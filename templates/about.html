Project overview

About NutriPrint

NutriPrint addresses school nutrition planning with a teacher-friendly platform for BMI assessment, meal generation, and printable health communication.

Problem statement

Schools need faster, clearer nutrition planning

Teachers often have to balance student health, regional food availability, print-friendly communication, and budget constraints without a single integrated tool.

Solution

A multi-page school health-tech workspace

NutriPrint brings BMI assessment, meal planning, food catalog browsing, and analytics together in one modern SaaS-style application.

What the platform aims to improve

Faster intervention

Spot underweight and overweight students early.

Clear communication

Share printable posters and reports with parents.

Regional relevance

Use foods that fit Karnataka regions and budgets.

Actionable analytics

Track plans generated and BMI distributions by class.

Technology overview

Built for a modern web workflow

  • Frontend: FastAPI templates, Tailwind CSS, responsive Jinja pages.
  • Backend: FastAPI routes, Supabase auth and storage, meal generation services.
  • Analytics: BMI distribution, dashboard stats, and nutrition gap summaries.
  • Print output: Poster PDFs and public plan routes for sharing and scanning.

Team information

Built as an academic interdisciplinary project

NutriPrint V2 is developed for school nutrition planning, teacher workflows, and user-friendly health communication in Karnataka.
